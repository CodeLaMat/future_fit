# FutureFit Careers Product Brief

## Product Summary

FutureFit Careers is a GDPR-compliant, AI-supported career transition and labour-market intelligence platform for highly educated unemployed professionals.

The internal MVP is designed for employment specialists. It helps specialists work with anonymised client groups, compare those groups against labour-market opportunities, draft client-ready communications, and record human review decisions before any recommendation is used.

## Primary Users

- Employment specialists who support groups of highly educated unemployed professionals.
- Programme coordinators who monitor service delivery, outcomes, and quality.
- Administrators who manage users, roles, audit trails, and platform configuration.

## Initial MVP Audience

The first release is internal only. It must support anonymised client groups instead of named individual client records.

No client-facing portal, automated outreach, or direct personal profiling should be included in the initial MVP.

## Core Problem

Employment specialists often need to translate complex client backgrounds into practical next steps while also understanding shifting labour-market demand. This work is time-consuming, inconsistent across specialists, and difficult to document transparently.

FutureFit Careers supports that work by combining structured group information, curated opportunities, explainable AI-assisted matching, and mandatory human review.

## Product Principles

- Human-led: AI may assist with analysis and drafting, but specialists make decisions.
- Privacy-first: use anonymised or aggregated group data by default.
- Explainable: every AI-supported recommendation must include plain-language reasoning.
- Reviewable: outputs that could affect clients must enter a human review process.
- Auditable: important actions must be logged.
- Practical: the MVP should reduce specialist workload without creating a new administrative burden.

## MVP Value Proposition

For employment specialists, FutureFit Careers provides a single internal workspace to:

- View anonymised client groups.
- Maintain an opportunities database.
- Generate explainable matches between group profiles and opportunities.
- Review, approve, reject, or revise AI-supported outputs.
- Draft client-ready email text.
- Export tracking tables for programme reporting.
- Access GDPR and AI transparency information.

## Success Criteria

The internal MVP is successful if specialists can:

- Log in securely with role-appropriate access.
- Create and manage anonymised client groups.
- Add and review labour-market opportunities.
- Generate match explanations that are understandable and non-deterministic.
- Route sensitive outputs through human review.
- Export basic tracking data.
- Inspect audit logs for important system activity.

## Non-Goals For Initial MVP

- No named client profiles.
- No automated decision-making.
- No automated email sending.
- No production integrations with job boards.
- No client-facing portal.
- No predictive ranking of individuals.
- No background scraping of personal data.
